Fridge Town

This is a fun personal project I started in my apartment. I don't think it's applicable to any jobs (even though I did once work at a refrigerator startup), but I had a lot of fun with it and wanted to share. I drew buildings and roads onto isometric patterns that I printed onto magnet sheets, which allowed all the buildings to be easily connected with the same scale and perspective. After drawing a building I would cut it out and put it on our fridge. I documented each new addition, and even got friends and roommates to add to it too. Once I had enough pictures I turned it into a gif to show its progress over time. I documented the process in a thread on Twitter.
